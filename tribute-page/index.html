Kid Cudi

Scott Ramon Seguro Mescudi, American rapper

Here is some info about the kid:

  • Cudi first began rapping towards the end of his high school career

  • He moved to New York City to pursue a music career

  • Cudi's early work caught the attention of Kanye West

  • In late 2008, Cudi was revealed to be included in XXL's 2009 annual Freshman Class. He was featured on the cover alongside fellow up-and-coming rappers

  • Cudi's debut album Man on the Moon: The End of Day, was released on Universal Motown Records on September 15, 2009

  • In January 2010, Cudi released Man on the Moon: The End of Day's third and final single "Pursuit of Happiness", which was eventually certified platinum by the RIAA

  • In April 2011, while performing at New York City's Roseland Ballroom Kid Cudi announced he was launching his own record label. The new label, which he will be releasing his third studio album on is called Wicked Awesome Records


    • Born: January 30, 1984 (age 36) Cleveland, Ohio, U.S.
